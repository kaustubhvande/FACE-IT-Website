About

FACE-IT

FACE-IT was found in the year 2006, January with a dual aim of providing the budding engineers and innovators of the country a platform for sharing and spreading the idea of science and technology. FACE-IT, a brainchild of eminent professors and an actuated group of students of Computer & Information Technology Department of K.D.K.C.E., working on a non-profit basis.

FACE-IT organizes various technical and non-technical events and a marquee annual techfest named 'Fahrenheit'. Today, FACE-IT reaches out to over 5,000 students from more than 50 colleges from India and around 100 corporate and faculty members.
